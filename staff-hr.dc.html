Leave & PTO — quick answers
How far ahead do I request?
As early as you can — at least two weeks ahead for planned leave, so your team can cover Sundays and events.
Who approves it?
Your direct supervisor, in BambooHR. You'll get an email when it's approved.
Where do I see my balance?
BambooHR → My Info → Time Off. Balances update as requests are approved.
