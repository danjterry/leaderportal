Dear church family

As we step into a new year, our hearts are genuinely overwhelmed with gratitude for the family the Holy Spirit has formed at Citipointe. Your faithfulness, sacrifice, and generosity have never been in vain. We are not here by accident, and we are not moving forward without purpose. There is a cause.

As we look ahead, we sense a holy urgency stirring among us. Like David standing before Goliath, we are reminded of the question that still echoes through Scripture: "Is there not a cause?" We believe there is. We see a generation in need of truth, a city longing for hope, and a world waiting to encounter the true power and presence of Jesus Christ.

As you pray into this year, we invite you to ask the Holy Spirit how He is calling you to respond. If you have ever questioned whether your part truly matters, hear this clearly: it does. In God's Kingdom, no obedience is overlooked and no sacrifice is wasted. What you bring is not small when it is placed in His hands.

The harvest is ready, the opportunity is before us, and our God is faithful. Together, we will continue to unmistakably influence our world for good and for God.

"Now to Him who is able to do immeasurably more than all we ask or imagine, according to His power that is at work within us, to Him be glory in the church and in Christ Jesus throughout all generations, forever and ever. Amen." — Ephesians 3:20–21

For the glory of God and for the sake of the world,
Pastors Joel and Savannah Ramsey

faith, love, hope
Faith

Faith does not wait for certainty. Faith responds to calling — it still steps out on the water, trusting the promises of God and believing that obedience releases heaven's activity on the earth.

Love

Giving is not a transaction, it is an act of love. Love is revealed through action. When we give, we put love into motion — needs are met, pathways are opened, and hope becomes tangible.

Hope

Hope declares, "You have a future." The story is not finished. There is more ahead — more purpose to fulfill, more breakthroughs to see, more lives to reach.

This is the heart of Kingdom Builders. We are a people who believe God, love relentlessly, and give generously — a church that refuses to stand still when a cause demands a response.

The Call

The Gospel must be preached. Lives must be reached. Cities must be transformed. Nations must encounter Jesus. This is not someone else's responsibility — this is ours.

The Mission

Through Kingdom Builders, we advance the Kingdom of God locally, nationally, and globally — investing in missions, outreach, leadership development, and future expansion.

The Response

Right now, each of us is invited to respond. Not based on comparison, but on conviction. Not measured by amount, but by obedience. What the Holy Spirit asks of you is your act of faith.

The Commitment

This is where belief becomes action. When you commit through Kingdom Builders, you are saying yes to the cause of Christ — trusting God to do immeasurably more through a surrendered people.
