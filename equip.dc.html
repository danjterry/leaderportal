Three-part class · with Freedom Collective

Spiritual Warfare

This three-part class, hosted in collaboration with Freedom Collective, will equip you with biblical truth, spiritual insight, and practical tools to recognize the enemy's tactics, walk in freedom, and live from a place of victory. Through Scripture-based teaching, discussion, and prayer, you'll gain clarity, confidence, and courage to navigate spiritual challenges with faith and wisdom.

What you'll learn
  • The reality of spiritual warfare and our authority in Christ
  • How to recognize and resist the enemy's strategies
  • How to stand firm using the Word, prayer, and worship
  • How to live daily from a place of victory and freedom

Whether you're new to faith or have been following Jesus for years, this class is designed to strengthen your foundation and empower you to live spiritually alert, grounded, and confident.

Practical Evangelism

Learn how to reach your lost family, friends, and complete strangers. You'll hear from Pastor Joel Ramsey, who has traveled the world preaching the good news of Jesus, and James Thayer, who regularly takes the gospel into the streets of Tennessee. We'll dive into the ins and outs of daily evangelism — teaching you how to preach the gospel effectively, street evangelize, and share your testimony.
