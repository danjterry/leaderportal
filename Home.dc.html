Who we are

The people who'll welcome you

We're a nondenominational church — unashamed in our worship of Jesus, uncompromising in the Word of God, and relentless in our love for people.

Citipointe began in Brisbane, Australia in 1974 and is now a global family of churches. Here in Middle Tennessee, we're equipping the people of Jesus for the glory of God and for the sake of the world.
