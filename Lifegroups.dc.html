Our Heart

Our Life Groups exist to make disciples by gathering people around Jesus in homes throughout our city.

Following the example found in Scripture, we devote ourselves to God's Word, prayer, fellowship, the breaking of bread, and caring for one another — so that Christ is glorified and His love is made visible to the world.

The Table

Most groups start with dinner

We're leaning into dinner parties — because something happens around a shared meal that doesn't happen anywhere else. Conversation slows down. Guards come down. Strangers become family.

Jesus did some of His best work around a table, and so do we: breaking bread, taking Communion together, and remembering our risen Savior.
